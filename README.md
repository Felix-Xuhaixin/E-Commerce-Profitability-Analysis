# **E-Commerce Profitability Analysis**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

 In this project I analyzed the data of products sale across multiple categories and channels. This type of analysis contributes to identify which product categories and sales channels are truly profitable after all costs, which marketing platforms are delivering the best return on ad spend, and whether the return rate is eating into margins

## 🎯 Objectives

- To identify the most profitable products category, find the specific costs that most significantly affect profit
- To analyze the profitability across the sales channels
- To identify the return rates by categories and channels
- To analyze the marketing spend data
- To make recommendations under the circumstances of cutted budget 

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- python : pandas numpy

---

## 🔍 Key Findings  

### 📌 Category Profitability  
- The highest profitable category is Electronics, and the lowest is Books;
- The product categories with lower profitability(e.g.Beauty, Books) have higher shipping cost, shipping cost is significant matter to the profitability 

### 🚨 Channel Analysis
- By channels, Mobile App has the highest average profit, and Marketplace has the lowest.
- For return rate, Social Commerce(9.14%), Mobile App(7.30%), Website(7.04%), Marketplace(6.44%)
  
### 🍽️ Marketing ROI
- By platform, Influencer is best performing, which delivers the ROAS 22.70:1; however, Email Marketing performs badly, which just delivers the ROAS 4.81:1. 

## 💡 Recommendations
- Reduce spend on low-ROAS platforms
- Minimize return-related losses
- Optimize shipping and discount costs
